Essential cookies

Required for the site to work. No opt-out — but no tracking either.

  • erplora_session — signed-in session, expires in 14 days.
  • csrf_token — anti-CSRF token, expires with session.
  • erplora-cloud-theme — your light/dark preference. Stored in localStorage, not a cookie.

Analytics

One cookie. Optional. Helps us understand which features are used.

  • _ga — Google Analytics 4, anonymized IP, expires in 13 months.
