Who we are

ERPlora ("we", "us") is a software service operated from Spain. We process personal data as a controller for our website visitors and as a processor for our customers' Hubs.

What we collect

From visitors: anonymized analytics (page views, referrers), and any details you type into our contact form (name, email, message).

From customers: account email, business details, billing info (via Stripe), and any data you choose to put into your Hub.

Why we collect it

  • Provide and operate the service you signed up for.
  • Bill you accurately for the plan you chose.
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, alerts, password resets).
  • Improve the product and prioritize roadmap items.

Who we share with

Only sub-processors required to operate the service: AWS (hosting, EU regions), Stripe (payments), Cloudflare (CDN, DDoS), and SendGrid (transactional email). All under signed DPAs.

Your rights (GDPR)

You can access, correct, export, restrict, or delete your data at any time. Email privacy@erplora.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Retention

Active accounts: as long as you stay. Cancelled accounts: 30 days then permanent deletion. Invoices and tax-related records: 6 years (legal requirement).

Cookies

We use a small number of essential cookies and one analytics cookie. See our Cookie Policy for the full list.

Questions about this policy? Reach us at legal@erplora.com.
