Our story

ERPlora was born from a simple idea: the software you run your business on should bend to fit your operations, not force you to bend to fit it. Every business is different — every shop, restaurant, workshop, agency, service provider has its own rhythm, its own quirks, its own non-negotiables.

Traditional ERP systems force everyone into the same workflow. ERPlora is built around a modular core: install only the modules you need, configure them to match how you work, and add more as your business grows.

What we believe

  • Software should be honest. No hidden per-user fees. No surprise transaction commissions. One plan, all modules, unlimited users.
  • Vendor lock-in is a tax on your future. Your data is yours. Export it any time, in standard formats.
  • Modern stacks make better products. ERPlora runs on Django 6.0, PostgreSQL, Redis, and a real-time WebSocket layer. Fast, reliable, and built to last.
  • Universal features should be free forever. If a feature helps the whole community, we build it once and everyone benefits.

Built in the open

ERPlora's marketplace is a living ecosystem. Universal modules — POS, inventory, CRM, invoicing, scheduling, attendance — are free, open, and continuously improved. Private modules let businesses encode their unique logic without paying recurring fees forever.

Where we're headed

We're a small team that ships fast. New modules every few weeks, weekly improvements to the core, and a public roadmap. If you have an idea, tell us — chances are we're already working on it.
